Federation Introduction

The ASIAN Mix Boxing Federation represents an integrated fighting discipline that draws from boxing, kick boxing, thai boxing, martial arts, and self-defense traditions. The mission is to strengthen the next generation of athletes through structure, discipline, national participation, and international vision.

With a focus on accessible training for all communities, the federation platform is designed to inspire confidence, sportsmanship, leadership, and pride in Indian combat sport development.

Official Journey

The federation story is rooted in years of practical training, athlete preparation, and a commitment to creating a recognized competitive pathway for mix boxing. Under the leadership of Grand Master Rakesh Suresh Mhaskar, the organization was formally founded on 14 October 2016 at Dhatav, Roha, Raigad, Maharashtra, with the goal of expanding the reach of the sport.

Today the association stands for technical excellence, official governance, athlete growth, fair play, and a premium competitive culture.
