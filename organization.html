Constitution & Rules

The federation maintains a disciplined system of official rules, athlete conduct standards, fair play requirements, and event organization procedures.

  • Promote safe, regulated, and respectful competitive participation.
  • Maintain technical rulebooks for events, scoring, and athlete categories.
  • Support transparent administration and committee-based decision making.
  • Encourage athlete development, referee integrity, and training excellence.

The Indian Mix Boxing Federation is an art of fighting that combines multiple systems into one competitive identity. The mix boxing game initially started in Dhatav Khedya village for students from all backgrounds and later evolved into a recognized federation structure. On 14 October 2016, the federation was founded at Dhatav, Roha, Raigad, Maharashtra.

Official Referee

Referee panels are expected to uphold fairness, safety, discipline, and technical quality at every official event.

  • Match supervision and scoring consistency.
  • Rule enforcement and athlete safety.
  • Professional conduct and neutral officiating.
  • Coordination with technical committees during competitions.
